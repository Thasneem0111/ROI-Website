UX and Marketing: Can They Work Together?

At first, UX (User Experience) and marketing might seem very different. UX is about making things easy and enjoyable for people to use , while marketing is about promoting products and convincing people to buy. But in places like Qatar, where people expect alot and there is a lot of competition, UX and marketing can actually help each other a lot.
