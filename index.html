Digital marketing service vs. traditional marketing

With the top digital marketing agency as your partner, your business can get more sales compared to traditional marketing alone.
Here's why more and more businesses are investing in digital marketing:

Digital Marketing

Marketing through the internet

Examples: social media, email, search engines, etc.

Easy control of your spending

You can set a daily budget at exactly what it is and working (preventing losses).

Faster results

Get instant exposure within hours.

Easy to measure results

You'll know how many saw your ads, how many clicked, how many purchased, and more in real time.

Bigger audience reach

Get exposure for your business both locally and nationally at any target audience.

Highly customizable

You can easily change what doesn't work and refine your campaign targeting to similar prospects.

Allows for easier customer communication

You can easily communicate with your customers 24/7 through various online platforms.

Gives you control over who sees your ads

Your brand will be exposed to those who are actively searching for similar products or services.

Traditional Marketing

Limited to offline marketing

Examples: telephone, print, billboards, flyers, broadcasts, etc.

Higher costs

You pay a big price upfront without any guarantee results.

Longer waiting periods

Takes weeks or months to set up and get actual results.

Difficult to measure results

Limited tracking options, and you won't know exactly how many people saw your ads.

Limited reach

Limited customization and too expensive if you're aiming for the national level.

Can't be updated easily

Higher risk since materials can't be updated easily once it's printed, aired, or installed.

Communication is only one-way

Customers don't have an option to instantly connect with you upon seeing your advertisement.

Limited control over ad exposure

It's almost impossible to filter who can see your ads once it's been put out.

Frequently Asked Questions

How am I going to be billed for the digital marketing services I will avail?

This depends on what service you availed from us. For example, our SEO services are on a subscription basis, which will be charged to you at the beginning of the applicable month. Meanwhile, for our website development service, we require a 50% down payment, and the remaining 50% will be settled once we deliver all the inclusions of the package.

If you want to know more about our pricing model, feel free to book a consultation with us. Our digital marketing consultancy specialist will answer all your questions.

Where can I find a digital marketing agency near me?

Wherever you are in the United States, Top Position would be a smart choice. We are currently headquartered in West Palm Beach, Florida, but our reach extends nationally. Don’t hesitate to book a consultation with us if you want to know how we can cater to your service area/s.

Who owns the website you're going to build for my business?

You’ll own everything on your website and the website itself. Still, we will provide you with the convenience of having specialists manage everything, so you don’t have to worry about the technical aspect. This makes our web design services a great investment for your business, whether you decide to work with us long-term or at your discretion.

I'm not ready for a full package yet, can I choose just one service?

Of course! You’re free to sign up for our complete digital marketing package or simply choose only the services you need at the moment. At the same time, you can always upgrade with more services once you’re ready to expand your marketing efforts.

All our services are flexible and can be tailored to your needs. Let us know your marketing goals, and we will create a custom plan for your business.

What kind of report do you provide?

For our SEO services, we provide monthly reports to let you see your growth each month. For our advertising campaigns, we can provide you with daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and even yearly reports upon request. Rest assured that we will update you regularly on your marketing campaigns’ status.

How do you measure the results of your campaigns?

We have a robust analytics system where we monitor every campaign we launch for our clients. This is also included in the reports we will provide regularly.

How can I reach you if I have concerns about my campaigns?

Once you decide to work with us, we will add you to our communication platform so you can easily reach out to the project manager assigned to you. This way, we can also give you updates on the get-go, making everything easy and convenient.

How much do your digital marketing consulting services cost?

Feel free to check the packages available on our service pages. If you wish to get custom services, book a consultation with us, and we will provide you with a custom quote.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Results timeline varies by service type. PPC campaigns can show immediate results, while SEO typically takes 3-6 months to see significant improvements. Social media marketing usually shows engagement within weeks, and website redesigns can impact conversions immediately. We'll set realistic expectations during our consultation.
